**App Name:** Meeting Minute Maker

**Concept:**  
An easy-to-use web app that helps small teams and freelancers quickly create, organize, and share meeting minutes. It offers templates, real-time collaboration, and automated action item tracking with deadline reminders.

**Key Features:**  
- Pre-built meeting minute templates (standup, client meeting, project kickoff, etc.)  
- Collaborative editing with team members in real-time  
- Automated extraction of action items and deadlines  
- Integration with calendar apps to send reminders  
- Export minutes as PDF or shareable web links  
- Searchable archive of past meeting notes

**Why it's profitable:**  
- Small teams and freelancers often spend a lot of time manually documenting meetings.  
- Many existing solutions are complex or expensive, so a simple focused tool can attract paying users.  
- Subscription pricing ($5-$15/month) for premium features like integrations and increased collaborators.  
- Low operational costs, easy to scale.
