**Automated Meeting Summary Generator**

**Problem:** Professionals spend a lot of time taking notes during meetings, and sometimes important details are missed or notes are disorganized.

**Solution:** A web app that integrates with popular video conferencing platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams) to automatically generate concise meeting summaries and action items from the transcriptions.

**Key Features:**
- Upload or connect recorded meetings or live transcription.
- AI-powered summarization that extracts key points and action items.
- Easy sharing and export options (PDF, email, Slack).
- Searchable meeting history database.
- Optional integrations with calendar apps for scheduling follow-ups.

**Why it's profitable:**
- Subscription model targeting small to medium-sized teams.
- Saves time and increases productivity, a strong selling point.
- Can start with simple transcription file uploads before integrating live APIs.
- Potential to upsell analytics or advanced integrations.
