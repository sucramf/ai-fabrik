Sure! Here's a simple and potentially profitable micro SaaS idea:

**Web App Idea:**  
**"MeetSnip" – Automated Meeting Summary & Action Item Generator**

**Description:**  
MeetSnip connects to popular video conferencing platforms (like Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams) and transcribes meetings in real-time or from recorded meetings. Using AI, it extracts key discussion points, summarizes the meeting in a concise format, and generates a clear list of action items with assignments and deadlines.

**Why it’s profitable:**  
- Saves time for busy professionals by eliminating manual note-taking and follow-ups.  
- Increases productivity by ensuring clarity on tasks from meetings.  
- Can be sold as a subscription targeting small to medium-sized businesses and remote teams.  
- Minimal required maintenance after initial AI integration.  
- Opportunity to add premium features like integrations with project management tools (Asana, Trello) or advanced analytics.

**Basic Features:**  
- Meeting transcription via audio upload or live integration  
- AI-powered summary generation (~3-5 bullet points)  
- Action items extraction and task assignment  
- Export summaries to PDF or email  

**Monetization:**  
- Monthly subscription plans (e.g., Free tier with limited minutes, Pro with unlimited minutes and integrations)  
- Team plans for collaboration  
- Add-ons for additional storage or premium integrations
