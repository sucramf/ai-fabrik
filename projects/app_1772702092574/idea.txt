**Web App Name:** SkillSwap

**Concept:**  
A micro SaaS platform that facilitates quick skill exchanges and peer-to-peer microlearning sessions.

**How it works:**  
- Users sign up and list 2-3 skills they can teach and 2-3 skills they want to learn.  
- The app matches users for 15-30 minute video or chat sessions focused on skill exchange — you teach me graphic design basics, I teach you smartphone photography tips.  
- Optional in-app scheduling and reminders.  
- Users can rate sessions to build trust and reputation.  
- Monetization via a small monthly subscription for premium features (e.g., unlimited sessions, advanced matching, priority listings) or a small fee per session.

**Why it’s profitable:**  
- Very lightweight and simple MVP to build.  
- Targets the growing self-learning and remote work markets.  
- Builds community and network effects, increasing value over time.  
- Subscription or pay-per-use models generate recurring revenue.  
- Low operational costs, mostly automated matching and messaging.
