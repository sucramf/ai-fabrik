``` This simple web app lets freelancers or small business owners create custom-branded invoices with line items, client info, invoice dates, and payment details. It instantly generates a clean invoice preview. The UI includes: - Branding inputs (logo URL, primary color) - Client info inputs (name, email, address) with saved client support in localStorage - Dynamic invoice line items with add/remove controls - Invoice info like number, dates, and payment method - Invoice preview generated live on form submit This is a minimal starting point focusing on easy invoice creation and preview. Features like emailing invoices, reminders, payment integration, saving invoices, and reports could be added next with backend services or APIs. Let me know if you want help extending it with those!
