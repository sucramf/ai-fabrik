**App Name:** InvoiceSpark

**Concept:**  
A streamlined, easy-to-use invoicing tool tailored for freelancers and small business owners who need to create, send, and track professional invoices quickly without the complexity of full accounting software.

**Key Features:**  
- Create and customize invoices with your branding (logo, colors).  
- Send invoices directly via email with automated reminders for due or overdue payments.  
- Track status of invoices: sent, viewed, paid, overdue.  
- Simple payment integration options (Stripe, PayPal).  
- Generate reports on income and outstanding payments.  
- Save client details for quick invoice creation.

**Why it’s profitable:**  
- Freelancers and micro-businesses always need to get paid faster and easier.  
- Many existing tools are either overly complex or expensive.  
- Offer subscription plans starting with a free tier (limited invoices per month) and premium for more features and higher limits.  
- Upsell features like custom domains, advanced analytics, or multi-user accounts.
