Here's a simple HTML web app prototype that focuses on the core user flow: uploading notes or recordings and generating an AI-powered meeting summary. This demo doesn’t do actual transcription or AI summarization (which requires backend/APIs) but shows how the UI and basic interactions could work. ```html Meeting Summary Automator

Meeting Summary Automator

Meeting Summary

``` **How to use this demo:** - Pick a meeting from the dropdown to simulate importing from a calendar. - Or upload any audio/video file to simulate a recording upload. - Or type notes directly into the textarea. - Click "Generate Summary" to see a placeholder AI summary. - Share buttons are non-functional and show alert placeholders. This is a minimal front-end prototype ready to be connected to real calendar APIs, transcription & summarization AI, and sharing integrations via backend services.
