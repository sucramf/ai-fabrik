Idea: **Meeting Minute Minute**

**Concept:**  
A lightweight web app that helps small teams quickly create, organize, and share meeting minutes. Unlike complex project management tools, this app focuses solely on streamlining meeting notes with action items.

**Key Features:**  
- Quick meeting note templates (agenda, attendees, discussion points, action items)  
- Real-time collaborative editing during meetings  
- Automatic email summary of meeting minutes to attendees  
- Task assignment with deadlines integrated into notes  
- Searchable archive of past meeting minutes  
- Lightweight, minimal UI for fast use without distractions

**Why Profitable?**  
- Small teams and freelancers pay for productivity tools but don’t need overly complex or expensive suites.  
- Subscription pricing at $5–$10/user/month targets this niche effectively.  
- Low maintenance costs with simple features and minimal integrations.
