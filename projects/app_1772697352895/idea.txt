**App Name:** Meeting Minute Magic

**Concept:**  
A lightweight web app that helps teams quickly create, share, and organize meeting minutes and action items. It focuses on speed and simplicity, ideal for small businesses, freelancers, and remote teams who want to keep meetings effective without the overhead of complex project management tools.

**Key Features:**  
- Quick meeting minute templates (daily stand-up, client calls, project updates)  
- Real-time collaborative editing  
- Automatic action item extraction and assignment  
- Integration with Google Calendar and Slack for reminders  
- Searchable archive of past meetings  
- Export to PDF or email summaries  

**Revenue Model:**  
- Freemium: basic features free for small teams (up to 5 users)  
- Paid plans for larger teams with advanced features (e.g., unlimited users, deeper integrations, analytics)  

**Why It Could Be Profitable:**  
- Many teams and remote workers struggle to keep meeting notes organized  
- Existing comprehensive PM tools are often too heavy or expensive for small teams  
- Simple, focused tools with integrations tend to attract steady user bases willing to pay for productivity boosts
