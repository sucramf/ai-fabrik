Idea: **QuickInvoice** – A simple micro SaaS for freelancers to create, send, and track invoices in under a minute.

**Core features:**
- Create and customize invoices with your branding.
- Save client info for faster invoicing.
- Send invoices via email directly from the app.
- Track invoice status: sent, viewed, paid, overdue.
- Basic payment integration with Stripe or PayPal.
- Dashboard showing total outstanding and paid amounts.

**Why it’s profitable and simple:**
- Targets freelancers and solo entrepreneurs who hate bulky accounting software.
- Minimalistic, easy-to-use product requires small dev effort.
- Recurring revenue via monthly subscription (e.g., $5/month).
- Opportunity to add premium features (tax calculations, reports) later.
