**Meeting Minute Summarizer**

### Problem
People spend a lot of time writing notes and summarizing meetings. It’s tedious and often leads to unorganized or incomplete minutes.

### Solution
A lightweight web app where users upload meeting audio recordings or paste meeting transcripts, and the app automatically generates clear, concise meeting minutes with action items highlighted.

### Key Features
- Audio upload + automatic transcription (using third-party APIs like Google Speech-to-Text)
- Paste or upload raw meeting transcripts
- AI-powered summarization to extract key points and decisions
- Action items highlighting and easy export to PDF or email
- Simple user dashboard with history and recurring meeting templates

### Monetization
- Freemium model: Free tier with limits on transcription minutes and summaries
- Paid tiers for higher usage, team collaboration features
- Add-ons like calendar integrations, custom branding, or priority processing

### Why It Can Be Profitable
- Saves professionals, managers, and teams time on a repetitive, boring task
- Appeals to remote teams, consultants, freelancers, and busy executives
- Relatively low competition for lightweight, easy-to-use tools with AI summarization
- High perceived value means customers are willing to pay monthly
