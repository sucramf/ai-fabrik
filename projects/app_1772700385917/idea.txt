**Web app idea:**  
**"QuickInvoiceBot"** — an ultra-simple invoicing tool for freelancers and small service providers who want to generate professional invoices in under 2 minutes without account sign-ups or complex setups.

---

### Core Features:
- Generate clean, printable PDF invoices with your logo, service description, client info, and payment terms.
- Save invoice data locally in the browser (no account needed).
- Download or email invoices directly.
- Customizable templates and colors.
- Basic invoice numbering and date autofill.

### Why it’s profitable:
- Freelancers and solopreneurs constantly need quick invoicing without clutter.
- Charge a low monthly fee (~$5-7) for premium features (branding, multiple clients, email history).
- Keep a free tier with limited features to attract users.
- Simple enough for rapid development and minimal maintenance.
