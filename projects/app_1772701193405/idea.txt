App Idea: "Meeting Minute Maker"

Description:
A lightweight web app that helps teams quickly generate, share, and store meeting minutes. Users can create a meeting, add attendees, jot down key points, assign action items, and instantly generate a clean, shareable summary.

Key Features:
- Simple interface optimized for fast minute-taking during or after meetings.
- Action item tracking with due dates and assigned owners.
- Export options (PDF, DOCX) and one-click sharing links.
- Searchable archive of past meeting minutes.
- Integrations with Google Calendar and Slack for reminders and notifications.

Monetization:
- Free tier with basic features and limited meeting storage.
- Monthly subscription for unlimited meetings, advanced exports, and integrations.
- Team plans with user management and analytics on follow-up completion rates.

Why it could be profitable:
- Many small businesses and remote teams struggle with organizing meeting outcomes.
- Existing tools are often bulky or too complex.
- Recurring SaaS revenue opportunity with low operational overhead.
