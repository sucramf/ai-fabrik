**Web App Idea:**  
**"Meeting Minute Maker"**

**What it does:**  
A lightweight web app that helps remote teams create, share, and track meeting minutes quickly and efficiently. Users input meeting notes in a structured template, tag action items, assign owners, and set due dates. The app then automatically sends reminders and generates summary reports.

**Key Features:**  
- Easy-to-use meeting minute templates  
- Action item tracking with deadlines and notifications  
- Integration with calendars (Google Calendar, Outlook)  
- Export minutes as PDF or shareable links  
- Searchable archive of past meeting notes  

**Why it can be profitable:**  
- Targets remote teams and organizations increasingly reliant on virtual meetings  
- Saves time and increases accountability, making it valuable for managers and project leaders  
- Can offer a free tier (basic features) with premium subscription for advanced tracking and integrations  
- Relatively simple to build and maintain with clear value to customers
