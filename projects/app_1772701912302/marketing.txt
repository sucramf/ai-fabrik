## Marketing Plan for Meeting Minute Maker

### 1. **Target Audience**
- **Primary:** Remote teams, startups, small to medium businesses who rely heavily on meetings.
- **Secondary:** Project managers, team leads, HR, and operations teams who need meeting accountability.
- **Geography:** Start with English-speaking markets (US, UK, Canada, Australia) and expand later.

### 2. **Value Proposition**
- Save time on note-taking and follow-up with structured meeting minutes.
- Improve accountability with automated action-item tracking and reminders.
- Seamlessly integrate with existing calendars to fit naturally into workflows.
- Maintain organized, searchable archives for future reference.

### 3. **Core Messaging**
- “Make every meeting count – Capture, assign, and follow up in minutes.”
- “Less note-taking, more doing – Automate your meeting minutes.”
- “Turn meetings into action with effortless tracking & reminders.”

### 4. **Launch Strategy & Channels**

#### Pre-Launch
- **Landing Page:**  
  - Clear headline, value props, CTA for beta signup/newsletter.
  - Include explainer video or screenshots of key features.
  - SEO optimization around keywords like “meeting minutes software,” “remote team meeting app,” etc.

- **Content Marketing:**  
  - Start a blog focused on remote work productivity, meeting best practices, accountability.
  - Guest posts on management and remote work blogs.

- **Community & Social Media:**  
  - Engage in remote work, startup, and productivity groups on Linkedin, Reddit (e.g., r/remotework, r/productivity).
  - Announce upcoming launch and gather feedback.

- **Beta Program:**  
  - Invite early adopters to test and provide testimonials.

#### Launch  
- **Product Hunt Launch:**  
  - Prepare assets, engage with community, highlight unique features.

- **Email Marketing:**  
  - Send launch announcement to beta users and email subscribers.

- **Paid Ads (Small scale initially):**  
  - LinkedIn Ads targeting remote team managers.
  - Google Search Ads for targeted keywords.

#### Post-Launch Growth  
- **SEO & Content Expansion:**  
  - Develop guides (“How to run effective remote meetings”), case studies, tips to improve team accountability.
  
- **Partnerships & Integrations:**  
  - Partner with calendar providers, project management tools to co-market or bundle.

- **Referral Program:**  
  - Incentivize users to invite colleagues.

- **Webinars and Demos:**  
  - Host live sessions on effective meeting management, showcasing the app.

### 5. **Pricing Strategy**
- **Free Tier:** Basic templates, manual reminders, limited archives.
- **Premium Tier (Subscription):**  
  - Advanced tracking and notifications  
  - Calendar integrations  
  - Export and sharing options  
  - Searchable long-term archive

- Consider monthly and annual plans with discount incentives.

### 6. **Retention & Engagement**
- **Onboarding Emails:** Guide users through key features step-by-step.
- **In-App Tips & Nudges:** Highlight underused features like tagging action items.
- **Regular Updates:** Share product updates and improvement based on user feedback.
- **Support:** Provide responsive customer support and a knowledge base.

### 7. **Metrics to Track**
- Sign-up rate & activation rate (users completing first meeting minute)
- Daily/weekly active users
- Conversion rate from free to paid plans
- Churn rate & user feedback
- Referral program participation
