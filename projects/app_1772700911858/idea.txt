**App Idea: QuickInvoiceGen**

**Description:**  
A micro SaaS web app that lets freelancers and small business owners generate professional invoices in seconds. Users input client info, services, and prices, and QuickInvoiceGen produces a clean, customizable PDF invoice they can send immediately.

**Key Features:**  
- Simple form to enter client details and line items  
- Customizable invoice templates with branding (logo, colors)  
- Automatic calculation of totals, taxes, and discounts  
- Save client profiles for quick reuse  
- Download or email invoices directly from the app  
- Track invoice status: sent, paid, overdue  
- Basic analytics on earnings and invoices  
- Optional recurring invoicing for subscription clients

**Monetization:**  
- Free tier: create up to 5 invoices/month with basic templates  
- Pro tier ($5-10/month): unlimited invoices, customizable branding, email invoices, recurring billing functionality

**Why it’s profitable:**  
Many freelancers and small businesses avoid bulky accounting software but need a quick invoicing tool that’s easier and cheaper! QuickInvoiceGen fills that niche with minimal features and low subscription cost, attracting many users seeking simplicity.
