**App Name:** QuickPitch

**Idea:**  
A web app that helps freelancers and small business owners create professional, customized pitch decks and proposals quickly using pre-built templates and AI-assisted content suggestions.

**Key Features:**  
- Drag-and-drop pitch deck builder with customizable templates  
- AI-powered content generation for sections like company overview, problem statement, solution, market analysis, and financials  
- Export options (PDF, PowerPoint, Google Slides)  
- Collaboration feature for teams to review and comment  
- Analytics dashboard to track which proposals were opened and for how long  

**Why it’s profitable:**  
- Freelancers and startups constantly need polished proposals to win clients and investors.  
- Saves users time and improves success rates with AI guidance.  
- Monthly subscription with tiered plans (e.g., Basic with limited templates, Pro with all features)  
- Low competition niche but high demand for proposal tools with AI assistance.
