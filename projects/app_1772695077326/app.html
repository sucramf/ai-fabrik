This web app named **QuickPitch** includes:
- Three simple templates selectable via UI.
- Textareas for each pitch deck section.
- Buttons next to each section to fill with simple canned AI suggestions.
- Live preview of the full pitch deck content with template info.
- Simple PDF export via a print preview.
