**Micro SaaS Idea: "QuickQuote" — Instant Custom Quote Generator for Freelancers**

**What it does:**  
QuickQuote allows freelancers and small service providers (designers, writers, developers, marketers) to quickly generate professional, branded project quotes and proposals. Users input project details, select pricing templates, add terms, and export polished PDFs to send clients instantly.

**Key features:**  
- Easy input form for project scope, deliverables, and timelines  
- Customizable pricing templates (fixed, hourly, milestone-based)  
- Brand personalization: add logo, colors, and signature  
- Auto-calculated totals with taxes and discounts  
- Export quotes as PDF or shareable link  
- Save and manage quote history  

**Why it’s profitable:**  
- Freelancers and agencies constantly need to send quotes to land projects — saves time vs. manual document creation  
- Simple subscription model (e.g., $10/month for unlimited quotes and branding)  
- Low overhead and easy MVP to build and scale  
- Potential to upsell integrations (invoicing, contracts) later
