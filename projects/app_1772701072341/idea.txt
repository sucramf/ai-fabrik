**Web App Idea:** Social Media Bio Link Optimizer

**Overview:**  
A simple web app that helps users create a clean, professional, and clickable “link in bio” landing page optimized for Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and other platforms that allow only one bio link.

**Key Features:**  
- Drag-and-drop link rearrangement  
- Customizable themes and fonts for branding  
- Analytics dashboard to track clicks and referrals  
- Scheduled links (activate/deactivate links at certain times)  
- Video and image embed support  
- QR code generation for quick sharing  

**Why It’s Profitable:**  
- Influencers, freelancers, small business owners constantly seek better ways to showcase multiple links.  
- Can offer a free tier with limited customizations and a premium subscription ($5-10/month) for advanced features and analytics.  
- Low development and maintenance costs.  

**Monetization:**  
- Freemium model with premium plans  
- Affiliate integration for tools and products linked from bio pages
