- **Easy Editable Meeting Minute Templates:** Use pre-designed templates tailored for various meeting types or customize your own to suit your team’s style. This ensures every meeting is documented consistently.
- **Action Item Assignment & Reminders:** Assign tasks directly from meeting notes with due dates and receive email reminders, so nothing falls through the cracks.
- **Searchable Archive:** Quickly access past meeting notes with a powerful search function, making it easy to reference decisions and discussions at any time.
