A SaaS tool called "EventEase" — a simple event scheduling and reminder service for small businesses and individuals who host regular meetups, workshops, or community events. Users can create events, send automated reminders via email and SMS, track RSVPs, and integrate with calendar apps. The clear problem it solves is reducing no-shows and organizing recurring events without complex, expensive software. Subscription plans could be tiered based on number of events and attendees tracked.
