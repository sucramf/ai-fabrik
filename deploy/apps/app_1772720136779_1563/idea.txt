A SaaS tool called "MeetingRecap" that automatically generates concise, actionable meeting summaries from video or audio conference calls. Targeted at busy professionals and project managers, it records meetings via integrations with Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet, transcribes the conversation, extracts key decisions, tasks, deadlines, and questions, then emails a clear, prioritized recap to all participants. This saves time spent on manual note-taking, ensures alignment, and improves follow-through.
