A web tool called "QuickSplit" that instantly divides group expenses. Users enter the total bill and who participated, add any individual adjustments (like someone ordered a pricier item), and it outputs exactly what each person owes, including tax and tip. Simple, clear, and useful for friends dining out, roommates sharing bills, or travelers splitting costs. It can become a small SaaS by offering saved group profiles, payment reminders, and integrations with payment apps.
