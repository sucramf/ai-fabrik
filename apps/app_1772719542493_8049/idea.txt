A simple SaaS tool called "ReceiptSnap" that allows small businesses and freelancers to quickly scan, categorize, and store receipts and expense records in one secure cloud location. It auto-extracts key data (amount, date, vendor) with OCR, generates monthly expense reports, and integrates with accounting software. This saves time on manual entry, reduces lost receipts, and simplifies tax prep. Subscription pricing starting at $5/month for basic use.
