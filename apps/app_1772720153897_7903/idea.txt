A SaaS tool called "MeetingMinder" that automatically generates concise, actionable meeting summaries and task lists from recorded video or audio calls using speech-to-text and AI summarization. Targeted at remote teams and project managers, it eliminates manual note-taking, ensures clear follow-up, and improves productivity. Users upload or integrate with popular platforms (Zoom, Teams), get downloadable summaries and task assignments, and can track completion statuses within the app.
